CyberShuttle®

The TSMC CyberShuttle® prototyping service significantly reduces non-recurring engineering (NRE) costs across the broadest technology range (from 0.5μm to 2nm), with the most frequent launch schedule (up to 10 shuttles per month), all through a convenient online registration system. Since its inception in 1998, CyberShuttle® has delivered hundreds of multi-project wafers, encompassing thousands of devices.

New design prototyping costs have risen with semiconductor technology advances, creating an access barrier to next-generation technologies. CyberShuttle® services overcome this barrier by enabling multiple designs to share tooling costs through a multi-project mask set. This significantly reduces mask NRE costs for product prototyping and circuit characterization. CyberShuttle® also validates IP sub-circuit functionality and process compatibility, standard cell libraries, and I/Os.

The Results
  • Minimizes design risks
  • Slashes prototyping cost by up to 90%
  • Supports all popular state-of-the-art technologies
  • Offers flexible and convenient online services

If you are a TSMC customer, login to TSMC-Online™ or contact your local TSMC representative for the latest CyberShuttle® schedule.
