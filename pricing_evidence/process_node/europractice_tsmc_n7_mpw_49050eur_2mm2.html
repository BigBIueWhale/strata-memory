TSMC University FinFET Program

Discover TSMC University FinFET Program

EUROPRACTICE-member universities can now gain access to TSMC FinFET technologies to be leveraged for their research, education and teaching. Access can be obtained at special pricing through this newly developed program.

Thanks to the more than thirty years of close collaboration between the Europractice partner IC-Link by imec and TSMC, universities can immediately kickstart new projects using TSMC’s FinFET technologies.

The TSMC FinFET program is also open to universities in North America.

PROGRAM HIGHLIGHTS

Offering the Industry’s Most Successful FinFET Technology Family to Europractice-member Universities

  • Starting with TSMC N16 and N7 (including RF)
  • Tapeout and manufacturing services

 

Design Collaterals for Both Teaching and Research

  • N7 and N16 Design Collateral: For test chip MPW
  • N16ADFP (Academic Design Foster Package): For Teaching Purpose Only
More TSMC Technologies

ACCESS

To access TSMC FinFET technologies, please fill in the application form and return it to tsmcfinfetprogram@imec.be.

Applications will be reviewed and approved by TSMC, after which an NDA will be shared. Access will be granted through imec’s secure data-sharing platform.

Do not hesitate to contact us with any questions.
