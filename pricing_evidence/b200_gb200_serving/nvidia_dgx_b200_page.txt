                NVIDIA Blackwell FAQs
How much does NVIDIA Blackwell inference cost per million tokens?
NVIDIA Blackwell-powered systems deliver AI inference at $0.02 per million tokens on GPT-OSS-120B using NVIDIA TensorRT™-LLM, as verified by 
SemiAnalysis InferenceX benchmarks 
(Q1 2026). $0.02 per million tokens on GPT-OSS-120B represents a 5x improvement over Blackwell’s own launch-day costs, driven by continuous software optimization.
Is NVIDIA Blackwell more efficient than NVIDIA Hopper for inference cost per token?
The most important metric for AI inference TCO is the cost per token, or the price-performance actually delivered. According to 
SemiAnalysis InferenceX benchmarks
 as of April 2026, NVIDIA Blackwell-powered systems deliver inference at approximately $0.02 per million tokens at 55 TPS/user for GPT-OSS-120B using TensorRT-LLM—roughly 4.5x cheaper than NVIDIA Hopper-powered systems at $0.09 per million tokens with vLLM.
