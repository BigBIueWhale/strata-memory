As high-performance computing (HPC) and artificial intelligence (AI) applications become more complex, the demand for the most advanced high-speed networking is critical for extreme-scale systems.

The NVIDIA MQM9700-NS2R is a high-performance InfiniBand switch designed for exascale computing, AI, and hyperscale data centers. The MQM9700-NS2R stands as a cutting-edge solution for modern data centers and high-performance computing environments. With its 64 ports of 400Gb/s NDR InfiniBand, it delivers an unmatched 51.2Tb/s of total throughput, making it ideal for AI, scientific research, and hyperscale cloud applications.

The NVIDIA MQM9700-NS2R offers future-proof infrastructure for the most demanding computational challenges. The MQM9700-NS2R is not only a switch…it’s a strategic enabler for next generation workloads.

  • Processor: x86 Coffee Lake i3
  • System Memory: 8GB 2666MT/S DDR4 SO-DIMM
  • Storage: 16GB SATA m.2 SSD
  • Software: MLNX-OS
  • Power Supply: 2x 2000W (1+1) Redundant Hot-Swappable 80 Plus Gold Power Supplies
  • Rails: Rails Included
  • Performance: 400Gb/s Per Port
  • Connectors and Cabling: 32 Octal Small Form Factor Pluggable Connectors; Passive or Active Copper or Fiber Cable; Optical Module
  • Switch Radix: 64 NDR Non-BLocking Ports with Aggregate Data Throughput up to 51.2Tb/s
  • Condition: New Open Box
  • Shipping Time: 4-Day Lead Time
  • Warranty: 90 Day Warranty
